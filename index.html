About

We believe that a wedding is a once-in-a-lifetime event that should be photographed quietly, sympathetically, and without the photographer attracting attention to themselves. Out of respect for the day we prefer to take documentary pictures which are a true reflection of the wedding itself and which can be enjoyed for generations to come.

We’re umbrella carriers. We’re button-hole pinners. We’re wedding dress straighteners. Above all, we’re storytellers. You want your wedding day to be filled with a little bit of tradition, plenty of personal touches and a lot of fun. You’re surrounding yourself with your closest family and friends, and want to wow them from the first invite to the last photo. If you like our style, why not get in touch?
